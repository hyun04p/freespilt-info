Welcome to FreeSplit! We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information through the FreeSplit app.

1. Information Collection

When you use FreeSplit, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register for an account or contact us for support.
  • Transaction Data: When you use FreeSplit to split bills, we collect transactional information, including bill amounts, names of payees, and payment divisions.
  • Device and Usage Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access FreeSplit, including the operating system, device identifiers, and usage patterns.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the functionality of FreeSplit.
  • To improve user experience and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and sending notifications related to your account.
  • To ensure the security of our service and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing of Information

Freesplit does not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights and safety of Freesplit, our users, and the public.
  • With service providers who assist in operating the app, under confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Freesplit reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
